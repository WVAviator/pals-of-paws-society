Adoption and Transports
Image: A graphic showing few people but many animals. Caption: Pals of Paws Society works hard to find adopters for homeless pets in Northwest Mississippi. 
Image: A graphic of a laptop and social media icons. Caption: Our volunteers use social media and local networking to find potential adopters or rescue organizations. 
Image: A graphic of three houses. Caption: The best outcome for any homeless pet in our community is to find a local adopter. 
Image: A graphic of a van. Caption: When there either aren't enough adopters or if local shelters and rescues are full, our volunteers work with out transport partners to arrange transportation for these animals. 
Image: A graphic of many people and no animals. Caption: The animals are brought to other parts of the US and Canada where there is much higher adoption demand.
