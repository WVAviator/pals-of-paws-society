# Super*ball* Tailgate Spay and Neuter Fundraiser

Come purchase some amazing [Corky's BBQ](https://www.corkysbbq.com/) ribs for your Superbowl Sunday barbecue, and support Pals of Paws Society in the process! By purchasing your game day barbecue through this fundraiser, you'll be supporting our mission to help reduce pet overpopulation in our community so that every pet in Northwest Mississippi can have a home.
